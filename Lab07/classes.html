Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga

Intended for beginner and everyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranyama (breath work) and quided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Although designed for intermediate to advanced studetns, beginner are welcom to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement--you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically though yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice is can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
